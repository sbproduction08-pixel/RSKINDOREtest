Humara Safar

The Taste of Royalty in Mahalakshmi Nagar

✨ Shuruat (The Vision)

Indore ki galiyon mein swad toh bahut hai, lekin **Royal Street Kitchen (RSK)** ka sapna ek aise thikane ka tha jahan street food ko "Royal" andaaz mile. Humne Mahalakshmi Nagar se is safar ki neev rakhi, taaki har Indori ko shahi swad munasib daam par mil sake.

🔥 Jazba aur Swad

FSSAI aur Udyam registration ke saath humne har legal bariki ka dhyan rakha. Deeksha aur Urvashi ki mehnat ne RSK ko sirf ek food cart nahi, balki ek bharosa banaya hai.

👑 Agla Padav

Aaj humare paas 42+ items ka menu hai aur sapna hai Indore ke har kone mein RSK ka swad pahunchana. Yeh safar abhi shuru hua hai, aur aapka saath ise aur bhi "Royal" banayega.
